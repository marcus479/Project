HISTORY

2010

The Selangor Sports Club started as the Selangor Cricket Club at the Selangor in the early 2010’s.

2013

The Selangor Cricket Club was originally a single storey clubhouse. It was extended in later years into this two storey building situated next to the Municipal building. This club was a popular meeting place for the Selangor community, especially cricket enthusiasts.

2020

The Selangor Cricket Club moved to the present clubhouse in 2020 as the Penang Golf Club and then changed its name to Selangor International Club. The clubhouse was at that time a military hospital and was later converted into an officer’s mess when the Selangor General Hospital was opened.

2021-2024

The Selangor Sports Club was established.
